Purpose of site

This website is designed to demonstrate my understanding of internal links in Bootstrap, specifically page navigation. These paragraphs will explain a different aspect of site. As a simple example, this paragraph explains the purpose of the site.

Top

Navigation and Structure

The navigation of this site is implemented using basic anchor tags. You can click on the navigation bar at the top of the page to move to different divs and topics of the page. You can also return to the top at any time.

Top

Design

The design of this page is reasonably basic. It amounts to one top level div, two row divs, and seven nested divs. The navigation bar is always at the top, and the divs underneath always form two columns on large, medium and small screens, and a single long column on the smallest screen size.

Top

Content of site

The site content is very simple, amounting (for the front end) to a selection of pictures, several paragraphs, and an internal and external navigation bar. There are also links inside of the page that allow you to move to different paragraphs easily.

Top

Interactivity

The interactivity of the site is focused purely on the link structure and responsiveness. You can navigate inside of the page with the internal links, and resize the page to watch the colors and paragraphs change. You can also leave the page via an external link.
